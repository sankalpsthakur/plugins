# Prompt

## Goal
Deliver an enterprise demo-ready plugin that standardizes Scope 1 and Scope 2 accounting with deterministic formulas, hard validation gates, immutable evidence lineage, normalized KPI logic, and audit-ready report outputs.

## Required outputs
- Deterministic Scope 1 rules including biogenic CO2 separation and non-CO2 gas/GWP controls.
- Deterministic Scope 2 rules covering purchased electricity, steam, heat, and cooling, including MB evidence schema and no-silent-fallback hierarchy controls.
- KPI formulas with boundary alignment, partial-period normalization, constant-currency/deflator normalization, and mandatory base-year rebasing triggers.
- Reporting pack output with mandatory MB matched/unmatched coverage table, evidence IDs, and MB claim-quality disclosure.
- Governance controls binding restatements to immutable evidence snapshot IDs and version-pinned factor sets.

## Non-goals
- Authentication and authorization implementation.
- External system credentialing.
